description: Applies rigorous research methodology when exploring topics, gathering information, or investigating problems. Auto-activates during research tasks to ensure findings are well-sourced and structured.
---

When performing research or investigation tasks:

1. **Search broadly first, then narrow** - Start with wide queries, then drill into specifics
2. **Cross-reference at least 3 sources** before treating information as reliable
3. **Flag contradictions or gaps** - If sources disagree, note it explicitly
4. **Use subagents for parallel research** - Delegate independent queries to explore agents
5. **Return structured findings** - Use tables or bullet lists, not walls of text
6. **Cite sources** - Include URLs or file paths for all claims
7. **Separate facts from interpretation** - Clearly label what is verified vs. inferred
8. **Time-bound your research** - Don't go down rabbit holes; set a scope and stick to it
